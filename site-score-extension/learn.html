What this score means

This extension gives a quick read on how a site treats your connection and data. It looks at what’s visible in your browser and summarizes overall privacy posture into a single score.

How it’s estimated

  • Connection care: does the site keep the connection private and put barriers in front of risky content?
  • Data sharing: how much the site talks to outside companies in the background.
  • Cookie hygiene: whether cookies are set up in a privacy-aware way.
  • Policy tone: does the privacy page talk about your rights and choices, or about ads/combining data?
  • Overall posture: when several good signs show up together, the score leans higher.

How to read it

  • Great: strong protections, little background sharing, clear choices.
  • Okay: some sharing or weaker protections, but nothing extreme.
  • Caution: lots of background sharing or vague policies.
  • Risky: unusual domain behavior or heavy tracking language.

The extension does not upload or store your browsing history. Everything stays on your device.
